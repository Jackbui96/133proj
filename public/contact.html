Shop

In the Shop tab, you can:

1) Browse all items from MiniSafeway Store.

2) Add items to cart.

3) Click on item to learn more about product.

Aisles

In the Aisles tab, you can:

1) Browse all the categories of MiniSafeway has to offer.

2) Click on a category to display all items in that category.

Sales

In the Sales tab, you can see:

1) All the sales happening for the current week.

2) The old and new price of the products.

History

For the history tab to be active, you need to be signed in. Now you can see:

1) All the products you have viewed.

2) Add products previously viewed directly to your cart.

CheckOut

After you have added all the items you would like, the checkout function allows you to purchase all the items in your cart after reviewing and adjusting the quantity of the items you would like.

This page also calculates your grand total, and directs you to a page where you will be required to enter your:

1) Card information (to check out as guest).

2) Login information (to check out as a member).
